Angela's Recipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dougth:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • 1 ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filing:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons gound cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to yhe mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
